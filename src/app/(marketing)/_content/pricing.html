FAQ’s

Everything You Need to Know, All in One Place

Discover quick and comprehensive answers to common questions about our platform, services, and features.

How do I set up my payment app?

Download the app, create an account, and follow the guided setup. Add your payment method, verify your identity, and you’re ready to start.

What payments can I make?

We sure do. Our support team is available 24/7 via email and live chat. We also have a knowledge base with tutorials and guides.

Are there spending limits?

Yes, spending limits vary based on your account type and verification status. You can view or request higher limits in your account settings.

How do I manage payment methods?

Go to your account settings and select “Payment Methods.” From there, you can add, remove, or update cards and bank accounts easily.

How do I dispute a payment?

To dispute a payment, navigate to the transaction in your history, tap “Report a Problem,” and follow the steps.
